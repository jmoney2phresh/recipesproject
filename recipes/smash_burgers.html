Ohio Smash Burgers

You already know what's up


Burgers

  • 1 lb. ground chuck (80% lean, 20% fat)
    • kosher salt, fresh ground black pepper
  • 1 small yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp beef tallow

To Serve

  • 2 brioche buns
  • 8 dill pickle slices
  • 4 provolone slices
    • ketchup, mustard, burger sauce etc

Method

  1. Separate your 1 lb. ground chuck roast into four roughly equal balls.
  2. Over medium-high heat, melt 1 tbsp beef tallow in a cast iron or non-stick pan until smoking.
  3. Place your ground beef balls into the hot pan, working in batches if the pan seems crowded, and press down firmly with the flat side of a spatula. Once flat, season with kosher salt and fresh ground black pepper.
  4. Repeat for your remaining beef if necessary.
  5. Toast your brioche bun halves in the pan.
  6. Assemble your burgers, topping with onions, pickles and your preferred sauce.
